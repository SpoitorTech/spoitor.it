<b>To make a Roblox game with Roblox Studio, you need to:</b><br>
1. Create a Roblox account on the official website.<br>
2. Install Roblox Studio on your computer and launch it.<br>
3. View Roblox game examples or choose a template to start with.<br>
4. Build your game by adding objects, scripts, terrain, and other features.<br>
5. Test your game by clicking the Play icon or pressing F5 on your keyboard.
